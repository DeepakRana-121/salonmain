Terms & Policies

Cancellation Policy

We request a 48 hour notice of appointment cancellations. Anything less than 24 hours will result in a cancellation fee of $50 or up to 50% of the services scheduled.

Providing ample notice gives the stylist and other clients a chance to schedule an appointment during that time slot. We appreciate your understanding of this policy by providing plenty of notice when needing to adjust your schedule.

We are a Green Salon

We partnered with Green Circle Salons and recycle more than 90% of our waste. In 2019, Salon W alone recycled more than 2 tons of waste! For more details on this exciting movement to go green visit Green Circle Salons.

Each service includes a small fee to help cover the costs of being green and additional salon sanitation for the safety of our clients.
